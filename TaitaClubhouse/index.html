Out of school class

Taita Clubhouse is creative technology space for people aged 10-18 and are totally free to use!

Our mission is to provide a creative and safe out-of-school learning environment where young people work with adult mentors to explore their own ideas, develop new skills, and build confidence in themselves through the use of technology.
